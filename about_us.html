About the Authority



Institutional Mandate

The Testing Authority Central is the national agency which is responsible for te development, administration and evaluation of the standardized examinations. Our primary mission is to provide a transparent and merit-based selection process for academic admissions.

Standardization:
To implement a testing framework which is uniform and ensures compatiblity with all different regions and education backgrounds.
Integrity:
To maintain the highest levels of security and confidentiality in examination data management and delivery.
Efficiency:
To leverage advanced technological infrastructure for the seamless conduct of large-scale, high-stakes assessments.
Research & Development:
To continuously refine assessment methodologies through data-driven psychometric analysis.
