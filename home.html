Introduction

The National Selective Assessment Test (NSAT) has been established as an official, independent, and transparent examination registration platform designed to conduct efficient, standardized, and fair assessments for students seeking admission to premier institutions across diverse fields ,including Programming, Photography, Fine Arts, Design, Technology, and various other academic and professional disciplines. The NSAT is committed to upholding the highest standards of quality, integrity, and equity in the evaluation process.

The NSAT portal serves as the official gateway for students to register for their respective examinations in a timely, accurate, and secure manner. This platform has been developed with a mission to streamline the assessment process while ensuring transparency and reliability at every stage , from registration to result declaration.

The NSAT strongly believes in the values of quality, fairness, efficiency, and accountability in all academic and skill-based assessments. To uphold these values, NSAT remains actively engaged with its key stakeholders , including students, parents, teachers, academic experts, industry professionals, and partner institutions , to continuously improve the examination experience and maintain international standards of assessment.

We invite all eligible candidates and institutional partners across all fields and disciplines to make full use of this platform and contribute to a credible, merit-based, and inclusive assessment system.
